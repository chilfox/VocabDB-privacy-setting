All data created within the VocabDB app (including your vocabulary lists, study progress, and preferences) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

VocabDB does not integrate with any third-party services that would have access to your data. The app operates independently and does not share information with external services.

Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device updated and using appropriate security features like screen locks.

Because data is stored locally, it is your responsibility to back up your vocabulary data if desired. If you uninstall the app or reset your device, your VocabDB data may be lost unless you have backed it up through your device's backup system.
